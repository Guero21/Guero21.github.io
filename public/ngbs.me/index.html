Criminal charges should be pursued against perpetrators, ranging from kidnapping and psychological abuse to harassment, depending on the extent of their involvement.

Their licenses should be revoked, and they should face criminal charges for their complicity in these heinous acts. The State's Attorneys of Florida, California, and Nevada are urged to prosecute these individuals to the fullest extent of the law, in accordance with each state's respective statutes and ethical standards governing medical practice.

Nicholas Guthrie
